## Before You Ask

Before opening a new issue or discussion, please:

1. **Search existing issues** - Your question might already be answered
2. **Check the documentation** - Many common questions are covered in our docs
3. **Try the latest version** - Your issue might already be fixed

<Note>
  The more information you provide, the faster we can help you. Include your app version, operating system, and steps to reproduce any issues.
</Note>

## Reporting Bugs

Found a bug? Help us fix it by submitting a detailed bug report.

### What to Include

When reporting a bug, please include:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App Version & Operating System">
    Specify the exact version of Obsidian Chess Studio you're using (found in Settings → About) and your operating system.
    
    **Example:** `v2.6.9 on Windows 11` or `v2.6.9 on macOS Sonoma 14.2`
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Steps to Reproduce">
    Provide clear, step-by-step instructions to reproduce the issue:
    
    1. Open Obsidian Chess Studio
    2. Navigate to Analysis tab
    3. Click "Start Engine"
    4. Observe the error
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Expected vs Actual Behavior">
    - **Expected:** What you thought should happen
    - **Actual:** What actually happened
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Screenshots & Logs">
    Include screenshots or screen recordings if relevant. Error logs can be found in the app's log directory.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new feature or improvement? We'd love to hear it!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check existing requests">
    Search [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/luisrivasnoriega/Obsidian-Chess-Studio/discussions) to see if someone has already suggested your idea.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a discussion">
    Open a new discussion in the **Feature Request** category with:
    - A clear description of the feature
    - Why it would be useful
    - How you envision it working
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage with the community">
    Discuss the feature with other users and maintainers to refine the idea.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="Request a Feature" icon="lightbulb" href="https://github.com/luisrivasnoriega/Obsidian-Chess-Studio/discussions/new/choose">
  Start a discussion about your feature idea
</Card>

## Community Guidelines

When participating in our community:

- **Be respectful** - Treat others with courtesy and professionalism
- **Be constructive** - Focus on solving problems and helping others
- **Be patient** - Maintainers and contributors are volunteers
- **Follow our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/luisrivasnoriega/Obsidian-Chess-Studio/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)**

<Warning>
  Please don't use GitHub Issues for support questions. Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/luisrivasnoriega/Obsidian-Chess-Studio/discussions) instead.
</Warning>

## Response Times

<Note>
  Obsidian Chess Studio is maintained by volunteers in their free time. While we strive to respond quickly, please be patient. Complex issues may take longer to investigate and resolve.
</Note>

### What to Expect

- **Bug reports:** Triaged within 1-3 days
- **Feature requests:** Initial response within 1 week
- **Questions:** Usually answered within 1-2 days
- **Pull requests:** Reviewed within 1 week
